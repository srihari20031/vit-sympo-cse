Paper Presentation

The goal of a paper presentation is to inform and educate the audience about the details and findings of the study, while also engaging and capturing the audience's attention. In a paper presentation, the presenter typically uses visual aids such as slides or handouts to supplement their verbal delivery, and interactive elements like questions and discussions to enhance engagement.

General Guidelines

1. 150 rupees is the registration fee per head for the entire event.

2. For team events the each team member has to pay for individual with team name.

3. Only registered participants will be allowed to attend the event , on spot registrations will also encouraged.

4. It is mandatory for the participants to provide their college ID at the help desk for verification.

5. Proper attire should be followed by the participants.

6. A participant is restricted to participation in 4 number of events as long as the event timings do not clash. Two from Technical and Two from Non-Technical

7. All the events will commence at the scheduled time , participants are requested to be punctual.

8. Participants are requested to collect the event tags from the help desk present in first floor.

9. No refund on the registration fees will be encouraged.

10. Students should refrain from abusive language, obscene display and a good decorum will be appreciated.

11. Smoking, consumption of alcohol or any such substance is strictly prohibited inside the college premises.

12. Rules are subject to changes at the discretion of the management or the event organisers.
